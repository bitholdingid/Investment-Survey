The "5 Whys" Laddering

If they pick "Capital Protection":

  1. "Why is the guarantee important?" -> Don't want to lose Modal.
  2. "Why is keeping principal crucial now?" -> Need it for Renovation.
  3. "How would you feel if it wasn't there?" -> Like I failed my family. (Root: Fear of Failure)
